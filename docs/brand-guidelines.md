# HDPM Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity

### Logo
The HDPM windswept juniper tree logo represents the natural landscape of Central Oregon and embodies the company's commitment to quality property management.

**Usage:**
- Always maintain clear space around logo
- Minimum size: 80px width for digital
- Use on white or forest green backgrounds only
- Do not distort, rotate, or modify colors

### Color Palette

**Primary:**
- Forest Green: #2C5530
- Use for: Headings, CTAs, brand accents

**Secondary:**
- White: #FFFFFF
- Use for: Backgrounds, text on dark surfaces

**Accent:**
- Light Gray: #F5F5F5
- Use for: Subtle backgrounds, dividers

### Typography

**Primary Font:** Clean sans-serif (system fonts acceptable)
- Headings: Bold weight
- Body: Regular weight
- CTAs: Bold weight

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 42-48px
- H2: 32-36px
- H3: 24-28px
- Body: 16-18px
- Small: 12-14px

### Photography Style

**General Principles:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Clean, decluttered compositions
- Professional quality required
- Authentic property representation

**For Budget Properties:**
- Bright, welcoming atmosphere
- Focus on cleanliness and functionality
- Showcase included amenities
- Practical, lived-in appeal

**For Premium Properties:**
- Architectural details highlighted
- Luxury finishes emphasized
- Lifestyle positioning
- Aspirational, sophisticated feel

### Voice & Tone

**Brand Voice:**
- Professional yet approachable
- Knowledgeable and trustworthy
- Community-focused
- Service-oriented

**Tone by Tier:**

*Budget Properties:*
- Straightforward and friendly
- Value-emphasizing
- Practical and helpful
- "Your comfortable home in Central Oregon"

*Premium Properties:*
- Sophisticated and refined
- Lifestyle-focused
- Aspirational yet authentic
- "Luxury living in Central Oregon"

### Application to Instagram Carousels

**Consistency Requirements:**
- Logo placement: Top or bottom corner
- Color palette: Strict adherence to brand colors
- Typography: Clear hierarchy in every slide
- Photography: Professional, on-brand imagery
- CTAs: Clear, action-oriented, branded

**Quality Standards:**
- All text must be legible on mobile devices
- Images must be high resolution
- Branding must be visible but not overwhelming
- Information must be accurate and current

---

**These guidelines ensure HDPM's brand identity remains consistent across all marketing materials and platforms.**
